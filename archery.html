ARCHERY

Archery is one of the most precise and skill-based Olympic sports, requiring focus, strength, and accuracy to hit the target.

Recurve Archery

Recurve archery is the only discipline in the Olympic Games, featuring bows that curve away at the ends.

Compound Archery

Compound archery uses advanced bows with pulleys, providing increased accuracy and speed.

3D Archery

Using life-sized animal targets in a wooded setting.

Field Archery

Field archery takes place on uneven terrain with varying distances, testing adaptability.

Team Archery

A team-based format where archers compete together for combined scores.

Barebow Archery

Traditional archery without sights or stabilizers.
