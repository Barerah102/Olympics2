CYCLING

Cycling in the Olympics includes a variety of thrilling events such as road racing, track cycling, BMX, and mountain biking.

Road Race

A long-distance endurance race held on public roads, testing cyclists' stamina and strategic racing skills.

Track Cycling

Cyclists race on a velodrome, showcasing speed and strategy through various sprint and endurance events.

BMX Racing

A fast-paced event where riders navigate a short, obstacle-filled dirt track in a race to the finish.

Mountain Biking

A cross-country endurance race on rough terrains, testing riders' strength and technical skills.

BMX Freestyle

Riders perform aerial stunts and tricks on ramps and obstacles, showcasing creativity and skill.

Team Pursuit

A strategic team event where cyclists work together to complete the fastest time on the track.
