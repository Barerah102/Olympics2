WEIGHTLIFTING

Weightlifting is one of the most challenging Olympic sports, featuring various disciplines that test an athleteâ€™s strength, technique, and endurance.

Snatch

The snatch is a lift where the weightlifter lifts the barbell overhead in one continuous motion.

Clean and Jerk

A two-phase lift where the athlete first lifts the barbell to their shoulders (clean) before pushing it overhead (jerk).

Powerlifting

A strength-based competition featuring squats, deadlifts, and bench presses.

Deadlift

A test of maximum strength where the athlete lifts the barbell from the floor to a standing position.

Squat

An essential weightlifting movement where the athlete lowers and raises the barbell while maintaining control.

Bench Press

A lift where the athlete pushes the barbell upward while lying on a bench, testing upper body strength.
