GYMNASTICS

Gymnastics is a sport involving exercises requiring balance, strength, flexibility, agility, coordination, and endurance.

Floor Exercise

A routine performed on a 12m x 12m mat showcasing flips, jumps, and artistic movements.

Vault

A powerful event where gymnasts sprint towards the vaulting table and perform acrobatics.

Rings

An upper-body strength event performed on two hanging rings requiring great control.

Pommel Horse

An event requiring continuous circular movements with balance and strength.

Parallel Bars

A set of two horizontal bars used for swinging and balancing routines.

High Bar

A high bar event requiring daring swings, releases, and dismounts.
