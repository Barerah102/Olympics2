WRESTLING

Wrestling is a combat sport involving grappling techniques such as clinch fighting, throws, and takedowns.

Freestyle Wrestling

A wrestling style where athletes can use their legs for both offense and defense.

Greco-Roman Wrestling

A style that forbids holds below the waist, emphasizing upper body techniques.

Folkstyle Wrestling

A style popular in the United States, focusing on control and positioning.

Sumo Wrestling

A Japanese wrestling sport where wrestlers try to force their opponent out of a ring.

Catch Wrestling

A historical wrestling style featuring joint locks and submission holds.

Professional Wrestling

A theatrical form of wrestling combining athleticism and entertainment.
