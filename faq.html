Frequently Asked Questions

You can purchase tickets through the official Olympic ticketing website or authorized sellers announced by the Olympic Committee.

The guidelines include mandatory vaccination proof, testing requirements, mask mandates, and social distancing in venues.

The Summer and Winter Olympics are held every four years, alternating every two years.

The Olympics can be streamed live on official broadcasting platforms like NBC Sports, BBC, and the Olympic Channel.

The Olympics feature athletics, swimming, gymnastics, basketball, football, and newer additions like skateboarding and surfing.

The International Olympic Committee (IOC) selects host cities based on infrastructure, facilities, security, and financial commitment.

Michael Phelps holds the record with 23 Olympic gold medals in swimming.

Over 200 countries participate in the Olympic Games.

The Olympic motto is "Citius, Altius, Fortius," which means "Faster, Higher, Stronger."

The Olympic rings are blue, yellow, black, green, and red, representing unity among continents.
